# Product Guidelines

## Brand Voice & Tone
- **Creative & Approachable:** The voice should be warm and inviting, fostering a sense of community while showcasing professional creative expertise.
- **Concise & Punchy:** Deliver information clearly, avoiding jargon, and maintaining a high-energy "Modern Studio" feel.

## Visual & Aesthetic Principles
- **Aesthetic Integrity:** Strictly adhere to the colors, fonts, and layout ideas present in the redesign concept.
- **Subtle & Precise Animations:** Interactions must be smooth but not overwhelming. Focus on high-precision micro-interactions that enhance the "premium" feel.
- **White Space Utilization:** Maintain the airy, modern layout that the original design likely features.

## User Experience (UX) Standards
- **Intuitive Navigation:** Users should find their way naturally across the site, with clear calls to action.
- **Ultra-High Performance:** The "latest tech" must be optimized for near-instant load times and zero-lag animations.
- **Responsive Design:** A flawless experience across all screen sizes (mobile, tablet, desktop).

## Technical Compliance
- **Accessibility:** Targeted WCAG 2.1 AA compliance to ensure the site is usable for everyone.
- **Code Quality:** Prioritize clean, documented code using modern frameworks and performance-oriented patterns.
